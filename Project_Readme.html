Instruction Guide For Admin, Buyer, Seller & Professional

This application can be used for Four User Types such as: Admin, Buyer, Seller & Professional:

For Admin

  • Choose User Type "Admin"
  • Login with username & Password
  • There are three admins.
    (1) Username="John" Password="123456@a"
    (2)Username="Alex" Password="123456@a"
    (3)Username="Christine" Password="123456@a"
  • Now You will reach Dashboard Page
  • You can View Order by clicking Order button in right navigation
  • You can View Product by clicking Product button in right navigation
  • You can View Supplier by clicking Supplier button in right navigation
  • You can View Customer by clicking Customer button in right navigation
  • You can View Admin by clicking Admin button in right navigation
  • You can setting up the page by just clicking Setting button in right navigation
  • You can logout easily by just clicking logout button in right navigation
  • You can choose UserType by just clicking usertype button in right navigation

For Buyer

  • Choose User Type "Buyer"
  • Login Or Register
  • Click Product Image & Reach View Detail
  • Choose Quantity & Click Add To Cart Button
  • Click Shopping Cart Button in Header & you'll reach Shopping Cart Page
  • Then Click Proceed To CheckOut Button
  • In Checkout Page, you need to fill Billing Data To CheckOut & Then Choose Payment Method & Click Place Order
  • Congratulation! Checkout Process was successfully completed. Now You can see your voucher in this page
  • Click Next Button
  • Click Go To Home Page Button & Reach Home Page
  • In Home Page, You can also buy from other seller. Please scroll down to the page and you will see View More from other sellers button and click it!
  • Now You will reach Products from other sellers page and you can buy products as I mention above
  • In Home Page, You can also buy from other seller. Please scroll down to the page and you will see View More from our professional button and click it!
  • Now You will reach Products from our professional page and you can buy products as I said before

For Seller

  • Choose User Type "Seller"
  • Login Or Register
  • Fill Product Information & Choose File To upload Photo
  • Now Click Add Button To Store in database And Display in both buyer and seller page
  • Click logout button in navigation bar
  • Now You can choose UserType by just clicking usertype button in navigation bar


For Professional

  • Choose User Type "Professional"
  • Login Or Register
  • Fill Product Information & Choose File To upload Photo
  • Now Click Add Button To Store in database And Display in both buyer and professional page
  • Click logout button in navigation bar
  • Now You can choose UserType by just clicking usertype button in navigation bar
